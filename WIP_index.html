About Me!

Nice to meet you!

As a developer, I enjoy both front-end and server-side, but my heart truly lies in C#!

I'm a Seattlite, currently living in San Diego! I'm still new to the area, but I can definitely see living here for some time. I love to eat and laugh and explore, and for me, any combination of these will result in great memories!
